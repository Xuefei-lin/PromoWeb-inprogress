Game Accessibility
Stastics

A survey conducted by PopCap in 2008 (Figure.1) has shown that there are 20% of gamershave an impairment related to physical, mental or development disability. An additional survey discovered there are 9% gamers suffer from a loss of gaming experience, and 2% gamers unable to play a game because of their impairments.

Accessibility Foundation in Utrecht (Figure.2), discovered the hours gamers with impairments spend weekly on gaming. The lowest number is 1 hour, the highest number is 25 hours. On average, they will spend 10.3 hours on gaming each week.
